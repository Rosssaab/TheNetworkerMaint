How We Operate

Principles that protect the network.

Free for members

No membership fees, no premium tiers, no upsells. Browsing events and joining groups is, and will remain, free to professionals and business owners.

Fair for organisers

A flat 4% + 20p per booking. No setup fees, no minimums, no contracts. Organisers keep what they earn and can leave whenever they like.

Built for the UK

UK-focused, UK-supported, UK-paid. Every feature, partnership, and decision is shaped around how British businesses actually network and grow.

People first

Real humans, real conversations. We answer the phone, reply to emails, and treat the network as relationships, not just transactions.

Our Approach

Simple, fair, and properly useful for British business.

The networking sector is full of high fees, locked-in contracts, and platforms that prioritise themselves over the people using them. The Networker exists to do the opposite: a clean, transparent platform where the value flows back to organisers and members, and the costs are visible to everyone.
